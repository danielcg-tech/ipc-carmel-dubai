About Us

Our history, our faith, and the people who lead our congregation.

Church History

Established in Dubai · Indian Pentecostal Church of God

IPC Carmel, a branch of the Indian Pentecostal Church of God was started by Pastor Samuel C Daniel in 1996. A small but strong group of believers from India gather together to worship God. We thank God Almighty for sustaining us in the city of Dubai and enabling us to worship Him.

What We Believe

01

The Holy Scriptures

The revealed will of God, the all sufficient rule for faith and practice. The Holy Bible consisting of sixty-six (66) books including the Old and New Testament, is the inspired Word of God, a revelation from God to Man, concerning the will of God in all things necessary to our faith, conduct and salvation.

Hebrews 4:12; 2 Peter 1:20-21; 2 Timothy 3:15-17
02

The Triune God

In one God, eternally existing in three persons, namely the Father, the Son and the Holy Ghost.

Deuteronomy 6:4; Isaiah 43:10-11; Matthew 28:19; Mark 12:29
03

Jesus Christ

That Jesus Christ is the only Begotten Son of the Father, conceived of the Holy Spirit, and born of virgin Mary; that Jesus Christ died on the cross, was buried and raised from the dead; that He ascended to heaven and is today at the right hand of the Father as our intercessor.

Isaiah 7:14; Matthew 3:16-17
04

The Holy Spirit

In the Holy Ghost, the Comforter of life, who proceeds from the Father and the Son; who with the Father and Son together is worshipped and glorified.

John 14:16-17
05

Creation & Redemption

That man was created in the image and likeness of God, but fell by voluntary transgression and his only hope of redemption and salvation is in Jesus Christ; that salvation, new birth and justification are wrought by the grace of God and faith in the Lord Jesus Christ.

Genesis 1:26-31; Romans 5:12-21; 10:13; Titus 3:5-7
06

Evidence of Salvation

The inward evidence of salvation to the believer is the direct witness of the Spirit, and the outward evidence to all men is a life of righteousness and holiness.

Romans 8:16; 12:1-2
07

Water Baptism

In the ordinance of water baptism by immersion, in the name of the Father, and of the Son and of the Holy Ghost, for all who repent and believe Jesus Christ as Savior and Lord.

Matthew 28:19; Acts 10:47; Romans 6:4
08

Baptism in the Holy Spirit

All believers are entitled to and earnestly seek the Baptism in the Holy Spirit, evidenced by the initial sign of speaking with other tongues as the Spirit gives utterances.

Luke 24:49; Acts 1:4-8; 2:4; 1 Corinthians 12:1-31
09

The Lord’s Supper

Consisting of the elements of bread and the fruit of the vine, is a memorial of His sufferings and death and a prophecy of His second coming.

Luke 22:17-20; 1 Corinthians 11:23-32
10

Divine Healing

Deliverance from sickness is provided for in the atonement and is the privilege of all believers, and the anointing of the sick with oil for healing and the laying on of hands.

Isaiah 53:4; Matthew 8:16-17; James 5:14-16
11

The Church

The body of Christ, the habitation of God through the Spirit, with divine appointments for the fulfillment of His great commission.

Ephesians 1:22-23; 2:19-22; Mark 16:15-20
12

The Second Coming

In the pre-millennial second coming of Jesus Christ, the resurrection of those who are asleep in Christ, and the rapture of those who are alive — the blessed hope of the Church.

1 Corinthians 15:51-52; 1 Thessalonians 4:16-18; Titus 2:13
13

The Millennial Reign

In the appearance of the Lord Jesus Christ from heaven, the salvation of all Israel, and the millennial reign of Christ on this earth.

Romans 11:25-27; Revelation 19:11-16; 20:1-7
14

Final Judgment

That the devil and his angels, the beast and the false prophet, and whosoever whose name is not found written in the Book of Life will be cast into everlasting punishment.

Revelation 19:20; 20:10-15
15

The New Heaven & Earth

In His promise and look for a new heaven and a new earth wherein the righteous will dwell.

2 Peter 3:13; Revelation 21:1
